FL: Portfolio: Showcasing our UI Work (Finally)

by Rakibuzzaman Rakib
Jan 25, 2015

You’ve probably browsed our portfolio, and you’ve almost certainly seen our Dribbbling. But have you noticed what we’re NOT showing? What’s the saying about how the cobbler’s children have no shoes?

Since I spend most of my production time in the UI world, I’m very sensitive to having our UI work get the attention it deserves. Not merely from an outcomes standpoint but especially from a process standpoint, because that’s where the foundation for creating value is laid.

Our portfolio does an excellent job of showcasing our stellar branding work and our extensive brand strategy process, but we haven’t done much in the way of illustrating our experience and interface design work.

And so, in an effort to move toward that, we give you but a brief visual look at some recent projects from our UI team. Take a look at our work and let us know how we can help you with your UI needs.
